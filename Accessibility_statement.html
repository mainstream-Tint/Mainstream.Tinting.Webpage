Website Accessibility Statement

Mainstream Tinting

Last updated: October 4, 2025

Our Commitment

Mainstream Tinting is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities, in accordance with the principles of the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA).

1. General Accessibility Information

Mainstream Tinting strives to ensure that its services are accessible to people with disabilities, with the strong belief that every person has the right to live with dignity, equality, comfort, and independence.

We are continually working to improve the user experience for everyone.

2. Accessibility Goal and Standard

The Mainstream Tinting website aspires to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.2 Level AA. We approach accessibility as an ongoing process, dedicating resources to identify and resolve barriers.

Note on Design: Our website is built using clean, semantic HTML and high-contrast styling, which inherently promotes accessibility compliance and usability for a wide range of users.

3. Technical Status and Limitations

While we strive for maximum accessibility, there may be some areas where full conformance is not yet achieved due to dynamic content or reliance on third-party libraries.

  • Non-text Content: All primary images, logos, and icons include appropriate descriptive alternative text (`alt` tags) for screen reader users.
  • Keyboard Navigation: All main navigation and static links are fully operable via keyboard.
  • Videos: Videos on our site are for visual purposes only and do not contain audio. We recommend users contact us for a full text description of the video content if needed.
  • Dynamic Components: Complex interactive components built using JavaScript (like carousels or collapsible menus) are subject to ongoing review to ensure full ARIA compliance.

4. Feedback and Contact Information

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of the Mainstream Tinting website. If you encounter accessibility barriers, please do not hesitate to contact us.

How to Contact Us to Report an Issue:

  • By Email: mainstreamtint@gmail.com (Subject line: "Accessibility Issue")
  • By Phone: (703) 772-5185

We try to respond to feedback within 5 business days and will take immediate action to address any confirmed issues.
