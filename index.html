Have Questions?

Find quick answers below, or send us your project details for a free consultation.

While every project is unique, the cost for residential window tinting typically depends on the type of film selected and the total square footage of glass. Most projects see a full return on investment within 2-5 years through energy savings alone. We provide a free, no-obligation quote tailored to your specific windows and goals.

High-performance solar films can reject up to 80% of solar heat. This can translate to savings of up to 30% on summer cooling costs by reducing the strain on your A/C system. The impact is noticeable almost immediately on both your comfort and your utility bill.

Not at all! Modern window films are designed to let in plenty of natural light while targeting specific wavelengths of solar energy (like infrared heat and UV rays). We offer a wide range of films, from nearly invisible to lightly-shaded, and we'll help you find the perfect balance for your needs.

Professionally installed architectural films are extremely durable. Most of our residential films come with a lifetime manufacturer's warranty that covers bubbling, peeling, and discoloration. Commercial installations typically come with a 10-15 year warranty. Our professional installation ensures you get the maximum lifespan from your investment.
