Which Film Is Right For You?

Choosing the right film depends on your primary goal. Use this guide to find the technology that best matches your needs.

"My room is an oven and the glare is unbearable."

You need maximum heat and glare rejection to make your space comfortable and usable.

Your Solution: Solar Film

"I want to protect my new floors, but I love the natural light."

Your goal is to stop fading, not sunlight. You need invisible protection that preserves the look of your windows.

Your Solution: UV-Blocking Film

"My bathroom window faces the street and I need 24/7 privacy."

You need a stylish film that provides 24/7 visual blocking while still allowing natural light.

Your Solution: Decorative (Frosted) Film

"I'm worried about storm damage or a break-in."

Reinforce your glass to hold shattered shards in place, delaying intruders and protecting from dangerous debris.

Your Solution: Safety Film

"I want our company logo on the glass conference room."

You have a specific design, logo, or pattern you want to apply to your glass for branding or style.

Your Solution: Custom Film

"My storefront glass keeps getting tagged or scratched."

You need a sacrificial layer that can be easily removed and replaced if vandalized, protecting the expensive glass below.

Your Solution: Anti-Graffiti Film
